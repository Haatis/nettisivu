Market Square

Info

The Oulu Market Square is the main market square of the city of Oulu, Finland.

The market square is located in the city centre, in the Pokkinen district, on the waterfront of the Oulujoki river.

Source: Wikipedia

Qstock

Info

Qstock is an annual two-day rock festival held at the end of July in Oulu, Finland.

The festival lineup includes some of the biggest names in both domestic and international music scene, regardless of the genre.
